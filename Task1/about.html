About InAmigos Foundation

Creating meaningful social impact through collective action and innovation.

Who We Are

A movement dedicated to transforming lives across India.

InAmigos Foundation, founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact.

With its base in Chhattisgarh, the foundation operates across India, addressing critical societal issues through a network of dedicated professionals and volunteers.

Our Credentials & Recognitions

  • ✔ Registered under Section 8
  • ✔ 80G & 12A Certified Ensuring transparency and tax exemptions.
  • ✔ CSR-1 Registered Eligible for corporate CSR partnerships.
  • ✔ NITI Aayog Registered Aligning with national development goals.
  • ✔ IAF ISO 9001:2015 Certified Maintaining high-quality standards.

Our Key Initiatives & Impact

  • Project SEVA 50,000+ meals and clothing distributed.
  • Project BACHPANSHALA Supporting education and digital literacy.
  • Project JEEV Feeding 50+ stray animals daily.
  • Project UDAAN Women empowerment and menstrual awareness.
  • Project PRAKRITI 20,000+ saplings planted.
  • Project VIKAS Training 30,000+ interns in various domains.
